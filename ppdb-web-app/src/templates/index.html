Registration Instructions

To register for the PPDB, please follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Register" link to create a new account.
  2. Fill in the required information in the registration form.
  3. Submit the form and check your email for confirmation.
  4. Once confirmed, you can log in using your credentials.
